Instructions to get up and running for helping me test Stations


  • Download the official Metamask extension.
  • Follow the Metamask instructions. Make sure you write down and save your seed phrase.
  • We'll be using the Harmony network for our test stations. Navigate to https://stations.network/add-network and click on the Harmony button. It should automatically configure metamask to use the right network for you.
  • When you open Metamask, near the top there should be something that says "Account 1" and, under that, an abbreviated hexidecimal address. This is your Ethereum address. Clicking on that element of the extension will copy your address to the system clipboard.
  • Send me a text with your address and I'll send you some ONE (the currency used to pay for the (nearly free) transaction fees on Harmony.
  • Monitor your account balance and, when the ONE comes in, confirm receipt.
  • I haven't added a friendly UI to join a station so send me your desired screenname (this can always be changed) and I'll add you to "The Lotus Eaters".


Using "The Lotus Eaters" station

The instructions for usage are invariant of which particular station you're on but, since "The Lotus Eaters" will be our first/primary multi-user test stations, let's make it concrete, shall we?

When you visit "the lotus eaters" station page, click the "Connect" button. If, successful, it should make a "Compose" button appear. It should bring up a pop-up modal where you can enter a broadcast. Upon hitting the "broadcast" button, Metamask will pop up asking you to (a) sign the content of the broadcast, and then (b) accept the transaction. Just hit "ok" (or whatever) for both.

Assuming everything went ok, within 5 seconds you should get a popup from Metamask saying the transaction was confirmed. Asynchronous station updates aren't implemented, yet (very soon, tho!), so you have to refresh the page in order to view our broadcast.

THERE'S MORE

The stations pages (not the info pages) are responsive and it work quite well on mobile. In order to use stations on mobile (something I highly encourage), download the official Metamask Android or iOS app and put in your seed phrase. All the ONE from your desktop Metamask extension should be in there. Hit the hamburger (three horizontal lines) menu in the top left, and tap on "Browser". Then you can navigate to whatever station you'd like, connect, and compose broadcasts!

MORE?!

Keep your seed phrase a secret; it's the secret key in order to spend your ONE. Though, if it falls into the wrong hands, the financial impact is negligible (we're talking losing a few cents), with your seed phrase or private key, someone can impersonate you on a stations and say bad things. Keep it safe.
